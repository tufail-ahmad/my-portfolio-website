About Me

MERN Stack Developer!

I am an enthusiastic and dedicated MERN Stack Developer, eager to begin my professional journey in the world of full-stack web development. As a fresher, I bring a strong foundation in MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js, coupled with a passion for learning, building, and problem-solving.
Over the course of my academic journey and personal projects, I have developed a solid understanding of both front-end and back-end technologies. I’ve built several hands-on projects that demonstrate my ability to design intuitive user interfaces, implement robust server-side logic, and manage NoSQL databases effectively. My approach emphasizes writing clean, readable code and continuously improving through feedback and practice.

My skill set includes:

  • Front-End Development : React.js (with Hooks and basic state management), HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS
  • Back-End Development : Node.js, Express.js, creation of RESTful APIs, basic JWT-based authentication
  • Database Management : MongoDB, Mongoose, basic CRUD operations, data modeling
  • Tools & Deployment : Git, GitHub
I have also gained experience in version control, agile collaboration tools like Trello and GitHub Projects, and using Postman for API testing. I’m quick to adapt to new technologies and have a strong desire to stay up-to-date with industry trends in JavaScript, cloud platforms, and web development best practices. Though I am at the beginning of my career, I am confident in my ability to learn fast, work hard, and contribute meaningfully to any development team. I enjoy collaborating with others, asking the right questions, and constantly pushing my boundaries as a developer.
I am actively seeking internship opportunities, entry-level developer roles, or freelance projects where I can apply my skills, gain real-world experience, and continue growing as a full-stack web developer.
If you’re looking for a motivated, disciplined, and curious developer to bring energy and fresh ideas to your team — I’d love to connect.
